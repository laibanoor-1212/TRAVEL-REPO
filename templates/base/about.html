About Safar-e-Haram

Hajj and Umrah are important journeys for Muslims, but many people find it difficult to understand every step. Safar-e-Haram is a simple web platform that guides pilgrims with step-by-step instructions through text and videos. It also includes a verified marketplace where only licensed HGOs from Pakistan can list their travel packages, allowing users to compare and book safely.

Our Mission

To make Hajj and Umrah easier, well-organized, and spiritually focused by providing clear guidance, trusted travel packages, and essential location information for every pilgrim.

🕋

Our Vision

To become the most reliable digital guide for pilgrims worldwide, ensuring every pilgrim feels supported and confident throughout the journey.

Our Goal

Our goal is to bring all licensed Hajj & Umrah companies onto one trusted platform. Safar-e-Haram gives these stakeholders a shared space where they can upload and manage their different travel packages, making it easier for pilgrims to view and compare everything in one place.
